Healthcare Center Management

Manage multiple healthcare centers, including hospitals, clinics, and nursing homes, all within a single system. Organize and track key information for each center.

Healthcare Center Form View

Configure and manage individual healthcare center details, including name, type (e.g., Hospital, Clinic, Nursing Home), address, contact information (phone, mobile, fax, email), and website.

Healthcare Center Kanban View

Hospital Buildings Management Configuration

Organize and manage the physical structures of your healthcare facility. Keep track of different buildings, their codes, and the healthcare center they belong to for efficient infrastructure management.

Hospital Building

Add and manage detailed information for each hospital building. Associate buildings with specific healthcare centers and assign unique codes for easy identification and organization within the system.

Wards Management

Manage hospital wards efficiently. Keep track of ward names, the associated healthcare center, the building they are located in, floor number, type (e.g., Men Ward, Women Ward, Unisex), and their current state.

Ward Configuration

Configure individual ward details, including the associated healthcare center, building, floor number, and ward type. Additionally, manage the facilities available in each ward, such as telephone access, private bathroom, television, refrigerator, air conditioning, and more.

Bed Management

Manage hospital beds efficiently. Track bed names, the associated ward and healthcare center, the building they are located in, bed type, telephone number, and their current state (e.g., Free, Occupied, Not Available).

Bed Configuration

Configure individual bed details, including the associated healthcare center, building, ward, telephone number, reservation charge, and bed type. Easily manage the bed status (e.g., Free, Occupied, Reserved, Not Available).

Bed Status Management

Easily update and manage the status of individual beds. The system allows you to mark beds as Free, Reserved, Occupied, or Not Available, providing a clear overview of bed occupancy within the healthcare facility.

Operation Room Management Configuration

Efficiently manage your hospital's operation rooms. Keep track of operation room names, the associated building, and their current state (e.g., Not Available, Free, Occupied, Reserved) to optimize scheduling and resource allocation.

Operation Room Status

View the current status of all operation rooms, including their name, associated healthcare center, building, and availability (e.g., Not Available, Free, Occupied, Reserved). This provides a quick overview for scheduling and planning surgeries.

Operation Room Status Update

Update the status of individual operation rooms. From a free status, you can easily mark the room as Reserved, Occupied, or Not Available, ensuring accurate scheduling and preventing conflicts.

Apothecary Management

Manage your hospital's apothecary with efficiency. Organize and track different pharmacies within your healthcare centers, along with their location and contact information, streamlining processes for pharmacists.

Apothecary Information

View a comprehensive list of your hospital's apothecaries, including their pharmacy name, associated healthcare center, address (street, city, state, country), and website address, if available.

Apothecary Overview

Get a visual overview of the apothecaries within your healthcare system. See key details such as the apothecary name, associated healthcare center, location, and contact email at a glance.

Apothecary Details

View comprehensive details for each apothecary, including address, website, phone number, email, and a list of prescriptions managed within that pharmacy. These fields can be further customized to meet your specific business needs.

Domiciliary Units Management

Manage your domiciliary units effectively. Organize and track different units, their associated healthcare centers, locations, and contact information for streamlined home healthcare services.

Domiciliary Units Overview

Get a clear overview of the domiciliary units within your system. See key details such as the unit name, associated healthcare center, location, and contact email for easy access and management.

Domiciliary Unit Details

View complete information for each domiciliary unit, including the associated healthcare center, address, contact details, infrastructure details (like capacity, material, number of rooms, surface area, bathrooms), and available amenities.

Out-Patient Admissions

Manage out-patient admissions efficiently. Keep track of patient records, appointments, and relevant details for patients receiving treatment without being admitted to the hospital.

Out-Patient Records

Browse the list of out-patient records. Quickly view patient names and basic information for efficient management of patients receiving treatment without hospital admission.

Patient Information

Access comprehensive information for each patient, including personal details, contact information, family history, medical history, insurance details, lab tests, lifestyle, socio-economic background, evaluations, surgeries, and call logs, all in one centralized view.

In Patient Summary

Get a quick summary of a patient's medical journey, including the number of appointments, prescriptions, vaccines administered, admissions, evaluations, and surgeries performed, all easily accessible from their profile.

In-Patient Admissions

View a complete list of in-patient admissions, including patient details, admission type, admitting physician, healthcare center, building, hospitalization date, and discharge date.

In-Patient Admission Records

Browse the complete list of in-patient admissions recorded in the system. View essential details such as patient name, admission type, healthcare center, building, hospitalization date, and discharge date for effective patient management.

Vaccines Management Configuration

Manage patient vaccinations efficiently. Keep track of patient names, the vaccines administered, the administering physician, the date of vaccination, and the institution where it was given.

Vaccination Records

View a detailed list of vaccination records, including the patient's name, the specific vaccine administered, the dose number, the administering doctor, the date of vaccination, and the institution where it took place.

New Vaccination Entry

Create new vaccination records by entering details such as the patient's name, the vaccine administered, the dose number, the administering doctor, the date of vaccination, the institution, and any relevant observations.

Patient Call Logs

Manage patient communication effectively with call logs. Track call references, call types (e.g., Phone, Email, SMS), call dates, associated patients, the person in charge, and access detailed call information.

Patient Communication Log

Review the complete log of patient communications. See details such as the call reference number, call type (Phone, Email, SMS, etc.), date and time of contact, the patient involved, the person in charge of the communication, and access further details of each interaction.

Create New Call Log

Create a new patient call log by entering relevant information such as the patient's name, call type, person in charge, date and time of contact, and any extra details or notes about the communication.

Physicians Management Configuration

Manage your medical professionals efficiently. View a list of physicians with their names, roles, contact information, and associated departments for easy access and organization.

Active Physicians Directory

Browse the complete directory of active physicians. View their profiles, including names, specialties or roles, contact information, and department affiliations within the hospital network.

Physician Profile

View and manage comprehensive information for each physician, including their professional details, contact information, work schedule, availability, and organizational structure within the hospital.

Appointments Management

Efficiently manage patient appointments. View a comprehensive list including appointment IDs, patient names, urgency levels, appointment start times, assigned physicians, and the current status of each appointment.

Scheduled Appointments

Review the list of scheduled appointments, including the appointment ID, patient name, urgency level, appointment start time, assigned physician, and the current status of the appointment.

Appointment Details

View complete details for each appointment, including the appointment ID, patient name, urgency level, assigned physician, start and end dates, request date, and associated pricelist. Track the appointment's flow through different stages like Draft, Requested, Outcome, and Invoiced.

Prescriptions Management Configuration

Manage patient prescriptions efficiently. View a comprehensive list including prescription numbers, patient names, prescription dates, prescribing physicians, dispensing pharmacies, and the current status of each prescription.

Prescription Records

View a detailed list of all prescription records, including the prescription number, patient name, prescription date, prescribing physician, dispensing pharmacy, and the current status of the prescription.

Prescription Details

View complete details for each prescription, including the prescription number, patient name, dispensing pharmacy, prescribing physician, prescription date, and the list of prescribed medicines with dosage, unit, form, duration, and any notes. Track the prescription's status as Draft or Invoiced.

Patient Evaluations

Manage patient evaluations effectively. View a list of evaluations including evaluation numbers, patient names, evaluating physicians, appointment references, evaluation start dates, Body Mass Index (BMI) readings, and the type of evaluation conducted.

Evaluation Records

View a comprehensive list of all evaluation records, including the evaluation number, patient name, evaluating physician, associated appointment number, evaluation start date, Body Mass Index (BMI), and the type of evaluation.

Evaluation Details

View complete details for each patient evaluation, including patient information, evaluating physician, evaluation dates, associated appointment, evaluation type, chief complaint, a summary of findings (signs, symptoms, mental status, diagnosis), vital signs, anthropometry measurements, and any extra information.

Newborn Records Configuration

Manage newborn patient records. View a list containing the newborn's name, mother's name, sex, date of birth, discharge status, doctor in charge, length, weight, cephalic perimeter (CP), and any congenital diseases.

Manage Newborn Information

Efficiently manage newborn patient information. View and organize records including the newborn's name, mother's name, sex, date of birth, discharge status, attending doctor, and key physical measurements like length, weight, and cephalic perimeter, along with any congenital conditions.

Newborn Details

View comprehensive details for each newborn, including the mother's name, sex, date of birth, discharge status, attending doctor, physical measurements (length, weight, cephalic perimeter), any congenital diseases, and APGAR scores at different intervals.

Surgery Management

Efficiently manage surgical procedures. View a comprehensive list including surgery numbers, patient names, surgery types, scheduled start and end times, surgeons, anesthetists, associated health centers, and the current status of each surgery.

Surgery Schedule

Review the complete schedule of surgical procedures. See key details such as the surgery number, patient name, type of surgery, scheduled start and end date & time, urgency level, assigned surgeon and anesthetist, the healthcare center where the surgery will take place, and the current status of the surgery.

Schedule New Surgery

Schedule a new surgical procedure by entering essential details such as the patient's name, surgery type, urgency level, assigned surgeon and anesthetist, the healthcare center and building where the surgery will take place, and the designated operation theater.

Insurance Management Configuration

Manage patient insurance information efficiently. View a list containing the insurance provider's name, the patient covered, an alias for the insurance, the insurance code, the current status of the insurance, and additional relevant information.

Patient Insurance Records

View a list of patient insurance records, including the insurance provider's name, the associated patient, an optional alias for the insurance policy, the insurance code, the current status (e.g., Activated), and any additional relevant information.

Insurance Policy Status

View the detailed status of an insurance policy, including the current status (e.g., Activated), inclusion date, activation date, inactivation date, and suspension date. Also see information about the policy's creation and last update.
