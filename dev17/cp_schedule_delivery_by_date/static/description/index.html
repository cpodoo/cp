Sale Delivery By Scheduled Date

The Sale Delivery By Scheduled Date module allows you to manage and automate the delivery process based on the scheduled delivery date in Odoo 17.

It enhances the sales and inventory process by automatically generating and managing delivery orders. Every delivery record is tracked in the Stock Move History menu, giving you a clear and organized view of stock movements. This improves order tracking and streamlines inventory management by linking sales with stock operations seamlessly.
