Overview

Hotel Management Module helps us run our hotel smoothly by managing all staff such as managers, receptionists, maintenance managers, and maintenance workers. This module allows us to view events, add viewers, and attract more customers. It also includes vehicle management to engage travelers and help them explore local attractions and viewpoints.

Features

Room Reservation

Reserve hotel rooms based on required capacity and room type.

Service, Food & Vehicle Management

Order services, food, and vehicles as per need.

Event Management

Book slots for your favorite events.

Maintenance Management

Request maintenance work as needed.
